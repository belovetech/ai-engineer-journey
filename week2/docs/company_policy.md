# NovaPay Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

NovaPay operates as a remote-first company. All employees may work from any location within their country of employment. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM in the employee's local time zone. Outside of core hours, employees manage their own schedules.

A home office stipend of $1,500 is provided upon hire for equipment setup. An additional $500/year is available for ongoing office supply and ergonomic needs. Coworking space memberships up to $300/month are reimbursable with manager approval.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive:
- 20 days of paid vacation per year (accrued monthly)
- 10 paid sick days per year (no carryover)
- 12 paid company holidays per year
- 5 days of bereavement leave

Unused vacation days carry over up to a maximum of 30 accumulated days. Days beyond the cap are forfeited at year-end. Employees must take at least 10 vacation days per year.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 8 weeks of fully paid leave. Leave may be taken within 12 months of the birth or adoption. Gradual return-to-work arrangements (50% schedule for up to 4 weeks) are available upon request.

## Engineering On-Call

On-call rotations run Monday 9 AM to Monday 9 AM (UTC). Each engineer is on-call approximately once every 6 weeks. On-call engineers must respond to pages within 15 minutes during business hours and 30 minutes outside business hours.

Compensation for on-call:
- Weekday on-call: $200/day flat rate
- Weekend/holiday on-call: $400/day flat rate
- Incident response beyond 2 hours: time-and-a-half hourly rate

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice per year: mid-year (June) and year-end (December). Reviews include self-assessment, peer feedback (minimum 3 peers), and manager evaluation. Compensation adjustments are made annually in January based on the year-end review.

## Travel Policy

Business travel requires pre-approval from the employee's manager. Flights should be booked economy class for trips under 6 hours. Premium economy is permitted for flights over 6 hours. Hotel accommodations should not exceed $250/night in domestic locations or $350/night internationally. Per diem for meals is $75/day domestic and $100/day international. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of travel completion.

## Data Handling and Privacy

Employees with access to customer data must complete annual privacy training. Customer payment data must never be stored on personal devices. All work on production systems requires VPN access and multi-factor authentication. Data breaches must be reported to the security team within 1 hour of discovery via the #security-incidents Slack channel. Failure to report a known breach is grounds for termination.

## Code of Conduct

NovaPay is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive workplace. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are not tolerated. Reports can be made confidentially to HR at hr@novapay.com or through the anonymous reporting tool at ethics.novapay.com. All reports are investigated within 5 business days.
